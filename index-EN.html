Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney+?

Disney+ is the destination for Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star, and more.

What can I watch on Disney+?

Disney+ offers you a growing collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and movies
  • Timeless classics and recent hits from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • More entertainment with comedies, dramas, true stories, and much more
  • Heartwarming tales for all ages from Pixar’s talented artists
  • The entire Star Wars saga and a large part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe
  • National Geographic documentaries, special productions, and much more
How much does Disney+ cost?

You can choose from three Disney+ plans:

  • Premium (€15.99/month or €159.90/year): Resolution up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR. 4 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos sound where available. Movies and series ad-free.
  • Standard (€10.99/month or €109.90/year): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on supported content. Movies and series ad-free.
  • Standard with ads (€6.99/month): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous streams. 5.1 surround sound on supported content.

At the end of your billing period, your subscription will renew automatically for the same period at the then-current Premium price (as shown above), unless you cancel or choose another plan.

Which devices and platforms can I use with Disney+?

Disney+ is available on mobile devices, web browsers, game consoles, set-top boxes, and smart TVs. Click here for the full list of supported devices.

Does Disney+ require a commitment?

Disney+ is contract-free, and you can cancel anytime. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your paid period. To cancel, follow these 6 steps:

  1. Sign in at www.DisneyPlus.com
  2. Select your profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select your subscription
  5. Select Cancel Subscription
  6. Select Continue to Cancel
